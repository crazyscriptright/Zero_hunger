Medical Care for Stray Dogs

We provide essential medical care for stray dogs, including vaccinations, sterilization, emergency treatment, and regular health checkups. Our team of veterinarians and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure that every street dog receives the care they need.

Our Medical Services

Vaccination

Protecting dogs from deadly diseases like rabies and parvovirus.

Sterilization

Controlling stray dog overpopulation through neutering/spaying programs.

Emergency Treatment

Providing immediate care for injured or sick stray dogs.

Deworming & Parasite Control

Treating stray dogs for ticks, fleas, and internal parasites.
